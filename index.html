IRUGA — from the Rutooro word meaning origin or source — is a fabrication and innovation studio based in Fort Portal, western Uganda.

Founded by Olimi Archangel, the studio works in technical partnership with S-Tartex Group.

We work where electronics, materials science, and industrial design converge. Our focus is on objects, systems, and assemblies that are engineered to perform reliably in the real world.

Every project is guided by engineering depth — physics, materials, fabrication precision, and clear design intent. We choose measured solutions over flash, and technical rigor over noise.

PRJ-001 / Through-Wall Presence Detection
A passive human presence and breathing rate detector using ESP32-S3 WiFi Channel State Information. No cameras. No active emission. The antenna reads the room through solid walls by analyzing ambient WiFi signal distortion caused by micro-movements in the human chest.
