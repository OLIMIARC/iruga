001 — Studio

CLOSING THE GAP
BETWEEN THEORY
AND WORKING DEVICE.

IRUGA is a fabrication and innovation studio based in western Uganda.

We build working systems from first principles — embedded sensing, electromagnetic hardware, material science, industrial fabrication.

The gap between a physics textbook and a working device is smaller than most people have been shown. Closing that gap is the work.

We are building toward something larger — a physical place where engineering is practiced, taught, and turned into products. Where young engineers in this region can come and leave with capabilities no classroom nearby is currently teaching.

We are not there yet. But SPECTRE is proof we are on the way.

In technical partnership with S-Tartex — empowering youth with workforce-ready STEM skills.

The work is real. This is where it begins.
